Crispitos

Crispitos - Tex-mex at its finest.

Crispitos are a stuffed tortilla with seasoned ground beef and cheese, fried to perfection. Serve with salsa con queso.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil, divided, for frying
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 1/4 cup salsa con queso, divided, or to taste (optional)
  • 2 cups grated Cheddar cheese
  • toothpicks

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil to a skillet over medium heat. Add onions and cook for 2 minutes. Add beef and taco seasoning and cook until beef is brown and crumbly, about 5 minutes. Drain extra liquid from skillet.
  2. In a large skillet heat oil over low heat until hot.
  3. Meanwhile, spread a teaspoon of queso sauce onto a tortilla as "glue. Add 1/4 cup browned beef in the center and top with 2 tablespoons cheese. Roll up tortilla and secure roll with a toothpick. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  4. Carefully place rolled up tortillas into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Transfer to a wire rack with a slotted spoon. Serve with salsa con questo and enjoy!
